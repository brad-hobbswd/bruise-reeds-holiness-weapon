About the Author

Bradley Hobbs writes from a place of lived experience, faith, and deep conviction. His voice is shaped by seasons of silence, moments of misunderstanding, and a calling that refused to be extinguished. Through every chapter of his journey, one truth remained steady: God is one, and His name is Jesus.

His work is not theoretical. It is personal. Every page carries the weight of conversations never had, questions never answered, and wounds that were often hidden in the name of holiness. Yet through that process, a deeper understanding of grace, truth, and restoration was formed.

––––––––––––––––––

Bradley writes for those who have felt overlooked, misrepresented, or silenced in spaces that were meant to reflect Jesus. His words aim to bring language to experiences that many have struggled to explain, offering clarity, validation, and hope.

His message is rooted in the belief that holiness and humanity were never meant to be separated. That truth without love wounds, and love without truth misleads. That Jesus embodies both fully, and calls His people to do the same.

––––––––––––––––––

This work exists to restore voice, to challenge systems that harm, and to call the church back to the heart of Jesus. A heart that listens. A heart that sees. A heart that restores.

Bradley continues to write, speak, and advocate for those who have been bruised yet remain standing. His desire is simple and unwavering: that every person knows they are seen, known, and deeply loved by Jesus.
