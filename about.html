About the Author

A journey of faith, restoration, and pointing every heart toward Jesus Christ.

Bradley Hobbs writes from a place of lived experience, unwavering faith, and deep conviction. His voice has been shaped through seasons of silence, misunderstanding, loss, and restoration. While circumstances have often challenged his calling, they have never diminished the truth that continues to guide every page he writes: God is one, and His name is Jesus.

His writing is not simply theological discussion. It is deeply personal. Every chapter reflects lessons learned through difficult conversations, unanswered questions, and moments when faith was tested. Rather than allowing those experiences to produce bitterness, they became opportunities to discover God's grace, His faithfulness, and His power to restore what seemed beyond repair.

Bradley writes for those who have felt overlooked, misrepresented, rejected, or silenced in places that were meant to reflect the compassion of Christ. His desire is to give words to experiences many people struggle to explain while reminding every reader that healing begins when truth and grace walk together.

Throughout every work, he seeks to encourage believers to look beyond human failures and return their focus to Jesus Himself. The heart of every message is not criticism of the Church but a sincere invitation to rediscover the compassion, holiness, mercy, and love that Jesus demonstrated throughout His earthly ministry.

Bruised Reeds was written to remind readers that brokenness is never the end of the story. The same Savior who restores hearts also restores hope, purpose, and identity. Even the deepest wounds can become testimonies of His faithfulness when surrendered into His hands.

Bradley continues to write with one purpose in mind: to encourage people to draw closer to Jesus, to embrace truth with humility, and to remember that every person is seen, known, and deeply loved by Him.
