Bruised Reeds

Some wounds cannot be seen. Some voices have been ignored for so long that silence begins to feel normal. Bruised Reeds was written for those who have experienced hurt in places where compassion should have flourished, where faith was questioned, or where truth became difficult to speak.

These pages are not written to condemn the Church, but to point every heart back to Jesus Christ. His grace restores what people often damage. His mercy reaches beyond disappointment, rejection, and failure. Even the deepest wounds can become testimonies of His faithfulness.

Whether you are beginning your journey toward healing or simply searching for hope, my prayer is that these chapters remind you that your story is not over. Jesus still restores bruised reeds.
